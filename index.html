Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of AI solutions does Xavigin provide?

We build and integrate AI into web, mobile, and enterprise applications from predictive analytics and chatbots to recommendation systems and workflow automation.

Do you only work with large enterprises?

Not at all. We partner with startups, SMEs, and enterprises alike. Our solutions are scalable, so we can start small and grow with your business.

Timelines vary by project scope. A simple AI integration may take a few weeks, while complex enterprise platforms can take several months. We provide clear roadmaps before development begins.

Yes. We specialize in integrating AI into existing applications, CRMs, and ERPs without disrupting current workflows, ensuring a smooth transition.
